# Privacy Policy

[日本語](./privacy-policy.html)

Last Updated: May 14, 2026

## Introduction

"natsuki" (hereinafter referred to as "this app") is a photo editing application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in this app.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photo & Camera Access

This app requests access to photos and camera for the following purposes:

- **Camera**: To take photos for editing
- **Photo Library (Read)**: To select existing photos for editing
- **Photo Library (Save)**: To save edited photos

**Important**: All photos taken or selected are processed only on your device and are never sent to external servers.

### 2. Face Detection

The App uses Apple's Core Image framework (CIDetector) on-device to detect the location of faces within photos selected by the user, solely for the purpose of automatically positioning face stamps.

- **Information detected**: Only the rectangular region (position and size) of faces. No biometric data, facial geometry, or facial recognition data is extracted.
- **Processing location**: All face detection processing is performed entirely on your device.
- **Retention**: Detection results are held in memory only during the active editing session and are immediately discarded when the editing screen is closed.
- **Transmission and sharing**: No face data is saved to disk, transmitted to external servers, or shared with any third parties.

### 3. Advertising Information (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information to optimize ad delivery:

- Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
- Device information
- App usage information
- Ad interaction information

For more details, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 4. Other Permissions

As part of the advertising service, this app may request the following permissions:

- **Bluetooth**: For proximity marketing by advertising services
- **Calendar**: For event creation by advertising services

These features are used by advertising service providers and are not directly used by this app.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. Providing app functionality (photo editing features)
2. Improving app quality and fixing bugs
3. Delivering advertisements

## Information Sharing

Information collected by this app may be shared with the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob**: For advertising delivery

These services handle information according to their respective privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- You can change camera and photo library access permissions in device settings
- You can limit the use of the advertising identifier (IDFA) in device settings
- You can stop future information collection by uninstalling this app

## Data Retention

- Photo data: Processed only on device and not stored by the app
- Face detection data: Held in memory only during the active editing session and immediately discarded when the editing screen is closed
- Advertising data: Stored according to AdMob policy

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
