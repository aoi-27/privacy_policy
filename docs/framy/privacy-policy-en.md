# Privacy Policy

[日本語](./privacy-policy.html)

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

## Introduction

"Framy" (hereinafter referred to as "this app") is a photo editing application that allows you to create collages by combining multiple photos. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in this app.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photo Library Access

This app requests access to your photo library for the following purposes:

- **Photo Library (Read)**: To select photos for use in collages
- **Photo Library (Save)**: To save created collage photos to your camera roll

**Important**: Photos selected and edited in this app are processed only on your device and are never sent to external servers.

### 2. Analytics and Crash Reports (Firebase)

This app uses the following services to improve our service:

- **Firebase Analytics**: To analyze app usage
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: To detect crashes and errors and improve app stability

These services may collect the following information:
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage information
- Crash logs and error information

### 3. Advertising Information (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information to optimize ad delivery:

- Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
- Device information
- App usage information
- Ad interaction information

For more details, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 4. Other Permissions

This app may request the following permissions as part of the advertising delivery service:

- **Bluetooth**: For proximity marketing by the ad delivery service
- **Calendar**: For event creation by the ad delivery service

These features are used by the advertising service provider and are not directly used by this app.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. Providing app functionality (collage creation, photo selection and saving)
2. Improving app quality and fixing bugs
3. Analyzing app usage
4. Delivering advertisements

## Information Sharing

Information collected by this app may be shared with the following third-party services:

- **Google Firebase**: For app analytics and crash reporting
- **Google AdMob**: For advertising delivery

These services handle information according to their respective privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- You can change photo library access permissions in device settings
- You can limit the use of the advertising identifier (IDFA) in device settings
- You can stop future information collection by uninstalling this app

## Data Retention

- **Photo data**: Processed only on your device; not stored externally by the app
- **Analytics data**: Stored according to Firebase Analytics policy
- **Advertising data**: Stored according to AdMob policy

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed without notice. If there are significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date on this page. We recommend checking this page regularly.
