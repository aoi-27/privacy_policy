# Privacy Policy

[日本語](./privacy-policy.html)

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

## Introduction

"Photo Roulette" (hereinafter referred to as "this app") is a photo application that randomly selects photos from your photo album in a roulette style. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in this app.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photo Library Access

This app requests access to your photo library for the following purposes:

- **Photo Library (Read)**: To load photos from a specified album and display them in the roulette feature

**Important**: Photos loaded by this app are processed only on your device and are never sent to external servers.

### 2. History Data

This app saves the history of photos selected by the roulette on your device. This history data is never sent to external servers.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. Providing app functionality (photo roulette, history management)
2. Providing the share feature for selected photos

## Information Sharing

This app does not share any information with third parties. All data is processed only on your device.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- You can change photo library access permissions in device settings
- You can delete local data and stop future information collection by uninstalling this app

## Data Retention

- **Photo data**: Processed only on your device; not stored externally by the app
- **History data**: Stored on your device and retained until you uninstall the app

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

All data is processed only on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed without notice. If there are significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date on this page. We recommend checking this page regularly.
