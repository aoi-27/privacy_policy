# Privacy Policy

[日本語](./privacy-policy.html)

Last updated: June 9, 2026

## Introduction

"StockLog" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is an application for tracking purchase prices of subscriptions and manual purchases, and managing next-purchase-date reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Purchase Item Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device only for the purpose of managing purchase items:

- Item name, purchase location, purchase URL, and other item details
- Purchase type (auto-delivery subscription or manual purchase)
- Next purchase date and cycle (days) settings
- Price history (price, date, and memo per purchase)
- Stock count, notes, and item photo

This data is stored only on your device (via SwiftData) and is never transmitted to external servers.

### 2. Notifications

The App uses push notifications, with your permission, to send next-purchase-date reminder alerts. Notification scheduling is processed only on your device.

### 3. Advertising Data (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information to optimize ad delivery:

- Advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Device information
- App usage information
- Ad interaction information

For more details, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. Providing app functionality (recording and managing purchase items, prices, and next purchase dates)
2. Sending next-purchase-date reminder notifications
3. Improving app quality and fixing bugs
4. Delivering advertisements

## Information Sharing

Information collected by the App may be shared with the following third-party service:

- **Google AdMob**: For ad delivery

Purchase item data and price history are never shared with any third party.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- You can change notification permissions in your device settings
- You can limit the use of advertising identifier (IDFA) in your device settings
- You can stop future information collection by uninstalling the App

## Data Retention Period

- Purchase item data and price history: Stored only on the device and never transmitted externally by the App
- Notification data: Processed only on-device and never transmitted externally
- Advertising data: Stored according to AdMob policies

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed without notice. In case of significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. We recommend that you review this page periodically.
