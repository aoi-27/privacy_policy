# Privacy Policy

[日本語](./privacy-policy.html)

Last updated: January 15, 2025

## Introduction

"mozata" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is a photo editing application that allows users to apply mosaic effects to photos. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photos and Camera Access

The App requests access to photos and camera for the following purposes:

- **Camera**: To take photos and apply mosaic effects
- **Photo Library (Read)**: To select existing photos and apply mosaic effects
- **Photo Library (Write)**: To save edited photos

**Important**: Photos taken or selected are processed only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

### 2. Crash Reports (Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to improve our services. The following information may be automatically collected:

- Crash reports (for improving app stability)
- Device information (device model, OS version, etc.)

This information is anonymized and does not contain personally identifiable information.

### 3. Advertising Data (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information to optimize ad delivery:

- Advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Device information
- App usage information
- Ad interaction information

For more details, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 4. Other Permissions

The App may request the following permissions as part of advertising services:

- **Bluetooth**: For proximity marketing by advertising services
- **Calendar**: For event creation by advertising services

These features are used by advertising service providers and are not directly used by the App.

## How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. Providing app functionality (mosaic effect features)
2. Improving app quality and fixing bugs
3. Delivering advertisements

## Information Sharing

Information collected by the App may be shared with the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting
- **Google AdMob**: For ad delivery

These services handle information according to their respective privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- You can change permissions for camera and photo library access in your device settings
- You can limit the use of advertising identifier (IDFA) in your device settings
- You can stop future information collection by uninstalling the App

## Data Retention Period

- Photo data: Processed only on the device and not stored by the App
- Crash reports: Stored according to Firebase Crashlytics policies
- Advertising data: Stored according to AdMob policies

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed without notice. In case of significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. We recommend that you review this page periodically.
